> Proper gene expression requires the collaborative effort of multiple macromolecular machines to produce functional messenger RNA. As RNA polymerase II (RNA Pol II) transcribes DNA, the nascent pre-messenger RNA is heavily modified by other complexes such as 5′ capping enzymes, the spliceosome, the cleavage, and polyadenylation machinery as well as RNA-modifying/editing enzymes. Recent evidence has demonstrated that pre-mRNA splicing and 3′ end cleavage can occur on similar timescales as transcription and significantly cross-regulate. In this review, we discuss recent advances in co-transcriptional processing and how it contributes to gene regulation. We highlight how emerging areas—including coordinated splicing events, physical interactions between the RNA synthesis and modifying machinery, rapid and delayed splicing, and nuclear organization—impact mRNA isoforms. Coordination among RNA-processing choices yields radically different mRNA and protein products, foreshadowing the likely regulatory importance of co-transcriptional RNA folding and co-transcriptional modifications that have yet to be characterized in detail.

进行一个的翻译：正确的基因表达需要多个大分子机器的协同努力，才能产生功能性的信使 RNA。当 RNA 聚合酶 II（RNA Pol II）转录 DNA 时，新生的前体信使 RNA 会被其他复合物大量修饰，例如 5′ 加帽酶、剪接体、切割与多聚腺苷酸化机器以及 RNA 修饰/编辑酶。近期证据表明，前体 mRNA 的剪接和 3′ 端切割可以在与转录相似的时间尺度上发生，并且存在显著的交叉调控。在本综述中，我们讨论了共转录加工的最新进展及其对基因调控的贡献。我们重点阐述了新兴领域——包括协调的剪接事件、RNA 合成与修饰机器之间的物理相互作用、快速与延迟剪接以及核内组织——如何影响 mRNA 异构体。RNA 加工选择之间的协调作用会产生截然不同的 mRNA 和蛋白质产物，预示着共转录 RNA 折叠和共转录修饰可能在调控中具有重要性，而这些仍有待详细表征。

> The first step—50 end capping—occurs as the 15–17 nt long nascent RNA emerges from the exit RNA PolII exit tunnel, owing to the recruitment of the capping enzymes by the phosphory lated C-terminal domain(CTD) of RNA PolII and physical interaction with the core polymerase (Figure 1A).1,2,3 The resulting 7-methyl guanosine cap protects the immature nascent transcript as well as the mature mRNA from 50 to 30 exonucleases, binds to nuclear and cytoplasmic cap-binding proteins, assists in targeting RNA for degradation during surveillance steps, and recruits splicing and translation factors.

 > This structure has reinvigorated an exon tethering model previously proposed from work using an internal ribozyme in the pre-mRNA to cleave the nascent transcript.<sup>69,70</sup> In this revised model, the exon is tethered to the elongation complex via U1, which rides along the gene as the growing pre-mRNA is looped out until synthesis of the 3′ss.<sup>71</sup> Interestingly, a requirement for an interaction with RNA Pol II to retain stable U1 binding would permit only a single U1 to associate with the nascent transcript at a given time, and this may result in the ordered removal of introns in a 5′ to 3′ manner as has been observed for multi-intron genes.<sup>23,62</sup> Furthermore, screening thousands of sequence combinations found within coding genes revealed that a 5′ss consensus sequence in a nascent RNA stimulates RNA Pol II transcription, possibly due to the recruitment and interaction with U1, suggesting positive feedback between the assembling spliceosome and RNA Pol II.<sup>72,73</sup> This modulation of transcription may exist for other splicing factors as well; disease-associated mutations in the U2 snRNP component SF3b1 reduce elongation rate genome-wide by stalling early steps in spliceosome assembly, possibly physically holding back the elongating RNA Pol II.<sup>74</sup> Interestingly, CDK11—a kinase that modulates transcription—also phosphorylates SF3b1, and this phosphorylated state is characteristic of the active spliceosome.<sup>75,76</sup> Co-transcriptional spliceosome assembly may act as a mechanism to promote productive transcription in the synthesis of long introns by reducing backsplicing, which gives rise to circular RNAs or RNA Pol II backtracking or pausing.

这一结构重新激发了一个先前提出的外显子拴系模型，该模型基于在前体 mRNA 中使用内部核酶切割新生转录本的研究 [69,70]。在这个修订后的模型中，外显子通过 U1 拴系在延伸复合物上，U1 随着基因一起移动，而不断生长的前体 mRNA 则被环出，直到 3′ss 合成 [71]。有趣的是，稳定 U1 结合所需的与 RNA Pol II 的相互作用将允许在给定时间只有一个 U1 与新生转录本结合，这可能导致内含子按照 5′→3′ 的顺序被去除，正如在多内含子基因中观察到的那样 [23,62]。此外，对编码基因内数千种序列组合的筛选表明，新生 RNA 中的 5′ss 共有序列会刺激 RNA Pol II 转录，这可能是由于招募了 U1 并与之相互作用，表明组装中的剪接体与 RNA Pol II 之间存在正反馈 [72,73]。这种转录调节可能也存在于其他剪接因子中；U2 snRNP 组分 SF3b1 中的疾病相关突变通过停滞剪接体组装的早期步骤，在基因组范围内降低延伸速率，可能从物理上阻碍了延伸中的 RNA Pol II [74]。有趣的是，CDK11——一种调节转录的激酶——也能磷酸化 SF3b1，并且这种磷酸化状态是活性剪接体的特征 [75,76]。共转录剪接体组装可能作为一种机制，通过减少反向剪接（产生环状 RNA）或 RNA Pol II 回溯或暂停，促进长内含子合成中的高效转录。
